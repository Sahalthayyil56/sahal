Hello, I’m Muhammed Sahal T. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science. As part of my academic journey, I developed Care4U, a service-based web application designed to facilitate blood donation, medical equipment donation, and the sale of handmade crafts and artwork by individuals in care homes and welfare organizations. I built this platform using Django for the backend and HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript for the frontend, with MySQL as the database.
